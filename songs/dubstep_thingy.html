Riddim

This is a sound-design project that I took up to learn about bass design in Serum. I designed all synthesizer sounds myself and learned a lot about how sound works along the way. It was a very artistic project overall and I found it cool to have an electronic song where I can really say that it is 100% my work, because it is. A friend of mine gave me some great ideas on how to make the second drop sound kinda lika a trap song and I think it turned out amazingly different to the first drop
